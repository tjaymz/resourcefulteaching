Welcome to Resourceful Teaching!

My name is Amy Trujillo, education is my passion.

I'm the Assistant Principal and Gifted Coordinator at Osceola Science Charter School. I'm passionate about education and creating opportunities for all students to succeed, particularly in STEM fields.

Throughout my career, I've worn many hats in education. I've been a Gifted and ELL Advocate, a Science Teacher, and a Curriculum Coordinator. In each of these roles, I've focused on providing equitable access to a high-quality education for all students.

In my current role as Assistant Principal, I supervise and assess teachers, develop and manage the budget, and assist with the hiring process. I also oversee the school's Special Programs and serve as the Gifted Coordinator. In this capacity, I work to ensure compliance with state and district regulations, and provide high-quality services to gifted students.

I'm always looking for new ways to improve my practice and support the students I work with. I'm a lifelong learner and I'm committed to making a positive impact on the lives of my students.

Let me know if you have any questions!
